One of the constables asked in a low voice.

If it was a normal murder, Ning Qi would definitely be the first suspect, but who in Jiang Yin City didn't know the methods of the Black Tiger Gang?

As the gang leader of the Black Tiger Gang, the Left Army already had strong martial arts skills. With more than ten officers working together, it was impossible to capture him alive, let alone kill him without using weapons.

They were well aware of the background of the new Young Master Ning Qi, a useless scholar who would never do anything, and would never commit such a monstrous disaster.

Whether it's related to him or not, we can only find out when we meet him, but I'm more inclined to it that the Violet Palace Realm has hired an expert. No matter what the possibilities are, the Violet Palace is unable to get out of this matter, and today, you all have to open your eyes wide for me. Li Hu used the teacup's lid to brush the tea leaves in the cup, and then gently sipped. From the way he moved, it seemed that he often drank tea, appearing to be carefree and at ease.

This child was definitely strange!

Li Hu thought to himself.

From the moment Ning Qi entered the hall to the moment he opened his mouth, every single movement he made did not resemble the actions of a weak scholar. Furthermore, when he looked at the lackeys, he did not reveal the slightest hint of fear. He was a bit arrogant.

How was this the weak scholar rumored to be in the outside world? This gave Li Lihu a feeling as if he was facing the lord of Jiangyin City!

"Black Tiger Gang?" Do you think that my Violet Palace Realm would be mixed up with this sort of inferior trash? Why are you asking the Black Tiger Gang in my Violet Palace? "

Ning Qi glanced at Li Hu and said indifferently.

Lower Nine Paths?

"Regardless of whether the Black Tiger Gang is of the lower class or not, today, Black Tiger Gang's Sect Master Zuo Jun and the rest, including the Jiang Family's Patriarch Jiang Posha, have all lost their lives.
