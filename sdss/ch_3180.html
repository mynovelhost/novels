C3180 Deliver Yourself to My Door

"Brother Ning, that's the Lord of the Yellow Bell Tribe, a perfect paleoblast."

Wang Chao pointed at the Taurus cultivator with a leisurely expression.

Even if there was a slight difference in realm, they could still chat and laugh merrily. On the other hand, Lin Longhu and the other Dao Seeking Initial Stage cultivators did not get angry because of this, but instead laughed merrily and chatted with him, occasionally commenting on the shortcomings of the two sides in the competition.

"No wonder a few Immortal Lords and several dozen Tai Yi were dead, and they were able to cause the Saint De Dynasty to spit out a Shinro Stone Mine. This Taurus is truly proud of himself."

Ning Qi sighed.

The Golden Crow Ape looked at Ning Qi, "That is very normal, the existence of Hundred Clan List has such an effect. Otherwise, why would those races go all out, and want to move a position up there? Hundred Clan List were established by the Three Great Saints, so their judgement of the strength of every race was extremely accurate. There was a difference in the ranking, so there would definitely be a huge difference in strength as well. "

Wang Chao and the others could not help but nod their heads. They had lived in the Divine Luo Mystic Realm for many years.

All these years, the human race had always been outside of the rankings, but it was different from the Three Great Saints. Because the human race's strength was too weak, they shared a common fate with the other four races and thus did not have the qualifications to enter the rankings. It was precisely because of this that as long as it was a race of Hundred Clan List s, they could treat the human race with contempt.

"hectogram, what do you think the current strength of the human race can be ranked as?"

Ning Qi laughed.

A thoughtful look flashed across the Golden Crow Ape's eyes as it replied seriously, "With a Saint's strength, I'm afraid that four to five people in the middle Dao Realm wouldn't be his match. With such strength, he's already ranked around fifty in the Hundred Clan List."

The top thirty all had late Dao Seeking, and the majority of the races over that age all had powerful mid stage Dao Seeking Patriarchs. Therefore, after considering many aspects of the battle, the Golden Crow Ape Clan was given a ranking.

"Fifty?"

Hearing that, Wang Chao and the rest's minds shook, their faces slowly filled with excitement. They did not expect that the human race would suddenly change from being outside of the Hundred Clan List to become a clan of fifty or so Hundred Clan List, but the Hundred Clan List had not been updated, and only they knew that the strength of the human race was different from the past. All of this was because of Ning Qi's existence alone!

After half a day, the battles between the few big clans gradually came to an end, Lin You Qing had also went up on stage, but she could not perform well, she was absent-minded, and in a few moments she was defeated by the yellow bell tribe's heaven's pride level experts, thus Lin You Qing's expression became extremely ugly, and her gaze turned to Ning Qi from time to time.

"It looks like the blood elves have qualified this time."

Soul-Refiner looked at the graceful figure on the stage and lamented.

Although they were both of the Great Perfection of the Emperor Realm, they only needed three to five moves to defeat someone of the same level. She held a small, delicate bow that occasionally flowed with red light, but after many rounds, she still had not used it yet, and was only using it as a close-combat weapon.

"hectogram, in truth, what are the rules of this Sacred Family Heaven's Pride Battle? Don't tell me that we should just let the Hundred Clans below casually compete, and let them pick out the strongest heaven's pride level experts to go to Three Great Saints, and fight against the other heaven's pride level experts? "

Ning Qi was a little doubtful.

"There are restrictions to the rankings, and the higher the ranking, the more places there will be. For example, the Taurus s have fifty spots, and after obtaining fifty victories, they will be able to enter the Three Great Saints if these fifty places are gathered. But... If they are unable to gather fifty people, then they do not have the qualifications to enter the Three Great Saints. "

The Golden Crow's Claw replied.

"Doesn't that mean that the higher the rank of the race, the less worthwhile it is?"

Ning Qi was slightly startled.

"Exactly."

"Everything Three Great Saints has done is very fair. For example, humans and the other four races that have yet to enter the rankings. As long as one person is able to pass the examination, they would be qualified to enter the Three Great Saints."

"That's reasonable."

Ning Qi nodded his head slightly, it seemed that the Three Great Saints had considered those weak races, such as the human race, the Three Holy Grounds, the Five Great Sects, and the Nine Great Empires, who had sent out their strongest heaven's pride level experts, it was difficult for even one of them to pass, so the human race was especially magnanimous, as long as one person could pass the test, they would obtain the qualifications to enter the Three Holy Grounds.

"Hahaha, everyone, thank you."

The competition had already ended.

This time, the one who passed was the female cultivator from the Blood Elf Clan. The Blood Elf Clan member laughed loudly and cupped his hands in a pleased manner towards Lin Longhu and the other three.

Lord of the Yellow Bell Tribe's expression was not good, but he could only smile and nod. Among the other three Dao Seeking experts, two of them maintained a smile, because their race already had many disciples qualified. There was a high chance that they would be able to get to the Three Great Saints, so it was not a big deal if they failed.

The Heaven's Pride that he brought with him was the one who had the most chance of making it out of the Xuantai Zong, otherwise, he wouldn't have been led by the Great Clan Elder of Xuantai Zong himself. He didn't think that he would be defeated so miserably, even in a tribe like the yellow bell tribe, where there was only a paleoblast leading, there would be a Heaven's Pride whose strength was on par with Lin You Qing's, or even a little stronger. The female cultivator of the Blood Elf Clan was even more abnormal, compared to her, one was the earth, and one was the sky.

"Lin Longhu, I heard that the troops from the Three Holy Grounds have all been annihilated, the nine great empires do not seem to be impressive, only your five great sects are the slowest in terms of movement, and now that you have lost, the other sects are not too far away. Your human race should not have the chance to go on a pilgrimage this time."

Although he had failed, there were still countless tribes in the Taurus. There were experts everywhere, and it was not difficult for them to gather a total of fifty of the heaven's pride level experts. Right now, there were more than thirty of them.

"Hmph, I still have matters to attend to, so I'll be taking my leave first!"

Lin Longhu snorted, he cupped his hands at the four people and was about to leave, but then, Lin You Qing immediately said with a sound transmission: "Master, that rude Immortal Monarch is also present!"

"Oh?"

Lin Longhu's gaze immediately changed as it swept across the place and landed on Ning Qi's body. At the same time, Ning Qi was also about to get up.

Since the competition was over, it was time for him to do his job.

Wang Chao and the others were both excited and nervous. For so many years, this was the only time a human had the chance to be proud!

"Good boy, you previously insulted this noble one, but you escaped. Who would've thought that you would deliver yourself up to me now."

Lin Longhu bellowed, his voice was mighty, and seemed to be able to spread throughout the entire yellow bell tribe. He was not in a good mood, and right now, he had found a way to vent his anger!
