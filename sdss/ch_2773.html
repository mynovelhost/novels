According to Yan Zhibei's words, the things that the human world could associate with the Underworld were not just statues. There were times when there was a road, a river, a mountain, a stone pavilion, a rock, etc …

But even so, there were very few living people who could bring their flesh to the Underworld. Those who could not die and could return to the mortal world would have great accomplishments. After all, this could be considered a fortuitous encounter.

In such a large Underworld, the highest level of management was the ancestor of the Rebirth Hall, and that included the Phantom King of Fengdu. Even though he was the Spirit King of the Fengdu City, above him, there were still many old ghosts that were stronger than him, and the cultivation levels of these old ghosts might reach as high as three thousand years, five thousand years, not to mention the management levels of the Rebirth Hall, which were basically ten thousand years old ghosts, whose strength was even higher than the thousand-year-old ghost's!

Inside the Netherworld Kingdom, Ning Qi and Yan Zhibei were walking down a small path. This small path was a shortcut to the Rebirth Hall, but at the same time, it was also the most dangerous path to the Underworld.

"If we climb two more mountains, we will have a thousand-year-old ghost, whose strength is not weaker than yours."

Yan Zhibei said.

He felt that it was strange, Ning Qi's request was something he had never heard of before. Normally, the living would avoid the cave of these old ghosts, but Ning Qi had asked him to lead the way, specially coming to visit these old ghosts.

This was actually very normal, but what he found strange was that the group of old ghosts that were killed by Ning Qi, had all disappeared without a trace. They did not even have a trace of soul power remaining. But he did not dare to say anything, and could only guess Ning Qi's goal in his heart.

Since there were no benefits, why did he have to kill these old ghosts?

Yan Zhibei even suspected that Ning Qi probably had a grudge with an old ghost in the Underworld back then, and now, he was killed as soon as he saw a ghost!

"Then what are we waiting for?"

Ning Qi smiled slightly, and quickly ran in the direction that Yan Zhibei had pointed.

Yan Zhibei could only follow along helplessly, he had never thought of finding a chance to escape, but he was afraid of Ning Qi's god-like sword techniques, he did not dare make his decision, even when Ning Qi was facing him from the back.

"I already have more than 10,000 merit points. I can just gather 100,000 more and I'll be able to exchange for the Mo family's mechanical arts. Hehehe …"

However, this time, the system gave him a pleasant surprise. The Mo family's mechanical arts had a high chance of success, and it had the ability to repair that puppet's knowledge. If it could recover to its peak state, then its combat power would be equivalent to a level B Tai!

Even if it could not reach the peak, it was still a set of Grand Perfection of the Xuan Immortal, it was extremely useful to Ning Qi to have such a subordinate!

Previously, he was troubled, and tried to find where he could get 100,000 contribution points from. But now, this Underworld, was simply Ning Qi's paradise!
