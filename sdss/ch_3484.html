"Yi, please wait a moment, this senior brother."

Just as Ning Qi was walking towards the Mission Palace, a grey-robed outer disciple suddenly called out to him.

Seeing Ning Qi stop, the outer sect disciple quickly walked to, sized him up, and asked in satisfaction: "This senior brother, are you causal stage cultivator?"

"Exactly."

Ning Qi laughed.

The other party's cultivation and Perfection of the Dao Realm were unknown which courtyard in Jade Light Star Palace they were an outer disciple.

Seeing Ning Qi nod and admitting it, a hint of happiness flashed past the eyes of the outer sect disciple. After looking left and right, he said in a low voice: "Since Senior Brother is a causal stage cultivator, you should know about the Burning Karma Fruit?"

The expression in Ning Qi's eyes changed slightly. He could already feel with his divine sense that four gazes were still fixated on him and the outer court disciple in front of him. Those four people standing not far away should be the same as the outer court disciple in front of him.

"I naturally know about the Burning Fruit. What do you mean by that? Let's get straight to the point."

Ning Qi said indifferently.

"Senior Brother, I am Zhou Yuanchong, a disciple of the eighty-third courtyard. We have a mission on hand, but the difficulty of this mission has already exceeded the limits of what an outer disciple can handle, so we wish to invite you to join us."

Zhou Yuanchong said in a low voice.

"Hehe, you can give me the Burning Karma Fruit?"

Ning Qi laughed.

Zhou Yuanchong calmly looked at Ning Qi, "I don't dare to guarantee it, but there's a certain probability that I'll get a Burning Karma Fruit. If Senior Brother agrees to complete this mission with us, then the Burning Karma Fruit will belong to Senior Brother, and the rest will belong to us."

"Why me? There are countless causal stage cultivator s within the inner sect. Wouldn't it be safer to ask for an inner sect senior brother? "

Ning Qi said indifferently.

"Inner sect disciple brother …"

Zhou Yuanchong laughed, and his smile contained a hint of bitterness. He looked left and right, and after seeing that no inner sect disciples had passed by, he then lowered his voice and said: "If this news was found out by the inner sect senior brother, then this mission would not have us as a part of it, so …"

"They are indeed a little overbearing. However, aren't they afraid that I will end up with the same result if I were to find out?"

Ning Qi laughed.

Zhou Yuanchong smiled slightly, "Senior Ning would definitely not be like this, because we have interacted with Senior Brother Mo Xie before. With his character, his friends would not be so overbearing."

"Looks like you've recognized me since a long time ago."

Ning Qi laughed, "Let's find a place to talk, this is not a good place to talk."

If the other party really had news about the Burning Karma Fruit, Ning Qi wouldn't mind giving them a good fortune.

...

"We greet the Senior Ning."

Zhou Yuanchong and the other five outer court disciples were in a quiet stone room, cupping their hands and saluting Ning Qi. Their expressions were very respectful and cautious, after all, dozens of years ago, the scene of Ning Qi defeating Jiang Ziran on the Star Arena had left a deep impression on them.

"To make a long story short, you go ahead."

Ning Qi pointed to Zhou Yuanchong.

Zhou Yuanchong nodded and methodically explained the mission to them.

An hour later.

Ning Qi already started to understand why Zhou Yuanchong wanted to look for him, because his strength was stronger than normal Grand Perfection of the Causality Realm. And the mission they wanted to do this time was actually extremely simple, five Immortal Palace cultivators s had a thirty to forty percent chance of completing it, but there was actually a Star Monster s with a Grand Perfection of the Causality Realm that accidentally settled down at the location of their mission.

As a result, they had to find a Ranker who could contend against this Star Monster for at least the time it took for an incense stick to burn, and let them complete the mission. As for the Burning Karma Fruit that Zhou Yuanchong mentioned, it was only a guess, at that time they took a quick glance, and saw that the Star Monster was protecting some sort of spirit fruit.

However, they guessed that it was because of that fruit that the Star Monster had settled in this place!

"After talking for half a day, you guys are not sure if it is a Burning Fruit either. In this business, you guys are certain that you will be able to profit or lose. It is actually possible that I might end up losing everything, no?"

Ning Qi laughed.

They finally found a suitable Ranker to help them complete the mission. If Ning Qi rejected them, then that would be fine, but they were afraid that Ning Qi would spread the news, and with that, the mission would be ruined!

"Senior Ning, to be honest, I'm not sure about the chances of success, but I've seen the Burning Karma Fruit once and I know a little about its aura. There's a thirty percent chance that it's the Burning Karma Fruit, and it's about to mature!"

Zhou Yuanchong cupped his hands and said.

Ning Qi muttered to himself for a moment, and under the nervous gazes of the five, waved his hand, "Forget it, let me accompany you guys for a trip.

"Senior Ning, you, you agreed to it?"

Zhou Yuanchong was slightly startled.

"Are you going or not?"

Ning Qi laughed.

"Go, go, we're leaving now!"

...

When the six gray robed outer sect disciples left the Archaic Stellar Pavilion, they would not attract any attention. In fact, on their way out, they would only raise their heads to look at the six Star Monster s before continuing to sleep, doing what they needed to do.

A dozen or so days later.

Under Zhou Yuanchong's lead, Ning Qi and the others arrived at an extremely beautiful valley.

In the center of the valley, there was a huge tree that seemed to reach the heavens. The branches were like a waterfall that cascaded down vertically and were densely packed, so dense that not even sunlight could penetrate inside. Surrounding the tree, the air was also relatively cool, clearly showing that it was not an ordinary object!

"The Star Monster you were talking about, is inside the tree?"

Ning Qi said indifferently.

"…"

Zhou Yuanchong forced a smile: "Senior Ning, this tree is that Star Monster …"

"This tree?"

Ning Qi's eyes moved slightly as he used the system to check the properties of the Star Monster. The result revealed that it really was a Star Monster, and was a little similar to the demon eating vine from the Tide Tone Pavilion.

"Senior Ning, for the mission that we are about to complete, we must first enter a catacombs. This catacombs should be in the center of the valley … But now, the entrance has been blocked by this Star Monster and we are unable to enter. If you can attract it for a period of time, we can seize the opportunity to enter and complete the mission. "

Zhou Yuanchong said in a low voice.

"How long do you need?"

Ning Qi said indifferently.

"Two hours …" It would be best if it took four hours, the longer the better! "

Zhou Yuanchong said.

"Yes, get ready."

Ning Qi laughed lightly, then with both hands behind his back, he leisurely flew into the sky. After a few breaths, he approached the tree demon, who seemed to have sensed something, and the vertical branches started moving without wind, entering some sort of defensive state, as though it was warning Ning Qi to leave.

"A good dog doesn't block the road, can you move a bit?"

Ning Qi smiled.

Under the nervous gazes of Zhou Yuanchong and the rest, with a whoosh, all the branches rushed towards the sky like sharp swords, piercing towards Ning Qi. All the branches were surrounded by black fog, it was obvious that it was poisonous!
