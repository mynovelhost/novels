C1743 Immortal warlord body

The outer ring of the Buddha Country had already been occupied by the experts of the various great sects.

Countless Martial Cultivator passed through the various Buddhist Ring's Weak Area in front of their eyes.

There were even places that transformed the Buddhist Ring's Weak Area into a temporary passage.

Using the temporary passage, Cheng Feng easily returned to the Fifteenth Ring of Buddha.

After that, he muttered to himself for a bit, then once again went to the Desert Oasis place where he previously trained.

Very quickly, Cheng Feng arrived at his destination.

He raised his eyes to look around and found that the oasis was bustling with noise, it was actually filled with dozens of Martial Cultivator s.

However, these dozens of Martial Cultivator s did not have a high cultivation, and even the strongest cultivators only had Peak Fragmentation Stage.

When Cheng Feng arrived, he casually released a burst of Qi, causing the Martial Cultivator s to immediately become fearful.

Without Cheng Feng's reminder, he had already packed his things and moved out of the oasis.

Seeing that, Cheng Feng laughed, then flew back into the oasis.

"Next, I'll need to raise the level of the Wargod Armor. In order to avoid any trouble, I'll need to make some preparations."

Wargod Armor s were now comparable to Semisage s, and even Holy Artifact s.

Even if many Semisage were to land on it, they would be able to withstand it.

By fusing with the Ferrous Domain this time, it would give birth to a Holy Might, allowing it to become a saint with a single step.

He had officially entered the Semisage level, or perhaps even the Holy Artifact level!

That scene was definitely not ordinary.

When many Martial Cultivator saw it, they would definitely run over to watch. Some even coveted it and started to fight for it.

Under normal circumstances, Cheng Feng was obviously not afraid of others fighting over him, but the key thing was that Cheng Feng needed to personally participate in the leveling up of Wargod Armor.

If he had not been able to get away, he would have been finished.

Therefore, he had to prepare some precautions in advance.

After making his decision, Cheng Feng immediately took action.

First, he used the oasis's flowers, plants and trees as the foundation, constructing a crude Defensive Formation.

Following that, the God Soul rushed out and took out the void jug.

Hualala …

A total of six to seven hundred golden beetles flew out from the pot.

All of them were Heavenly Dragon Louse s, some of them had even reached the general level or even the king level.

"Looks like swallowing a large amount of Heaven-ascending Stage Martial Cultivator's and Earth Scale Giant Demon's corpses will cause the Dragon Lice's power to rise a lot!"

Cheng Feng laughed and muttered, "Very good, I hope you guys will perform even better!"

"Go, all of you, and lurk around the oasis."

"The moment any outsiders approach, I will expel them all!"

Following Cheng Feng's orders, whoosh whoosh whoosh …

The hundreds of Heavenly Dragon Louse turned into streaks of golden lightning.

In the blink of an eye, all of them disappeared.

After doing all of this, Cheng Feng then strode towards the lake in the center of the oasis.

Only after he had condensed a thick Dragon Strength barrier that covered the entire lake did Cheng Feng let out a long breath.

He took the Wargod Armor and the Ferrous Domain out from the Soul Ring.

The Wargod Armor was still completely golden, with dense ancient patterns on its surface. It gave off an indestructible feeling.

However, if one looked carefully, they would find that they were missing something.

To cause the entire set of armor to fall into a incomplete state … there was a sense of loss.

"Spirit, the Wargod Armor lacks spirit."

Cheng Feng muttered: "At the same time, there is also a lack of coordination, which is unable to integrate the power of the arms, legs, and torso into one body, making it unable to reach the peak!"

"Cheng Feng, the spirit of this armor, I deliberately chose not to nurture it."

At this moment, War God's voice sounded out, "At that time, this god forged this armor to ensure that nothing unexpected happened during the ninth heavenly tribulation."

"Heaven Calamity does not allow outsiders to interfere. If this armor gave birth to Artifact Spirit, the heavens would definitely think it was a living being."

"At that time, if I use this armor to defend against heavenly tribulation, instead of reducing the power of the divine tribulation, it will increase instead. It will only cause more trouble."

"So that's how it is." Cheng Feng nodded suddenly.

He finally knew why the majestic Wargod Armor didn't have any more Artifact Spirit.

"However, from what I know, you didn't borrow a Wargod Armor to face the heavenly tribulation in the end."

Cheng Feng said: "It seems that all the Heaven-ascending Stage's nine heavenly tribulation were forcefully withstood it with your body, and forged into immortal battle Divine Body!"

"Un, it is useless."

The war god nodded. "This is because this god has discovered that you don't need any external forces to go through heavenly tribulation in order to obtain the greatest amount of training. Your cultivation has increased the most."

"If you want to become the strongest Heaven-ascending Stage, it would be best if you don't borrow external power to go through the tribulation."

"In this way, after you pass through the heavenly tribulation of the ninth level, you will be able to smoothly step into the Creation Stage."

"It should be very difficult to cross the tribulation without using any external forces, right?" Cheng Feng raised his brows.

"Extremely large. It could even be said to have a slim chance of survival."

The War God said, "Also, you are most likely still talking to a top genius."

"If an ordinary Heaven-ascending Stage were to use their body to take the blows, it would simply be a form of suicide!"

"Indeed, there is no such thing as a free lunch in this world."

Cheng Feng's face twitched. "If you want to reap the rewards, then you must pay a corresponding price."

"Forget it, let me first raise the Wargod Armor to its strongest state!"

"As for the matter of transcending the Heavenly Tribulation, we'll talk about it when the time comes."

While speaking, Cheng Feng brought the Wargod Armor in front of him, and then picked up the buddhist awl to take a closer look.

"War God, how do I integrate this fifty kilograms of Ferrous Domain into the Wargod Armor?"

Cheng Feng asked: "After the flames melt, were they poured over the armor?"

"No, this method won't work."

The War God shook his head. "The Ferrous Domain is very sturdy, it would be extremely difficult for a Martial Cultivator of the Eighth or Ninth Layer to destroy it."

"Melting with fire, even King Grade Fire cannot burn it!"

"You can't even burn the King Grade Fire?"

Cheng Feng's brows furrowed greatly: "Then what method should I use to merge the Ferrous Domain into the Wargod Armor?"

"It's very simple. You don't need to do anything."

The War God laughed: "You just need to place the Ferrous Domain beside the Wargod Armor and the armor will slowly absorb the Ferrous Domain's essence."

"After the absorption is over, the initial stage of the Holy Might will be developed."

"If the Holy Might is complete enough, it's just a Holy Artifact. If the Holy Might is incomplete enough, it's a Semisage!"

The difference between Holy Artifact and Semisage mainly lies in the difference between the two.

The Holy Might had completely formed, and was the Holy Artifact.

The Holy Might had not fully formed, so even if it was a Semisage, it still had a flaw!

"Eh, I don't need to do anything?"

Hearing this, Cheng Feng was stunned for a moment before speaking, "You only need to put the Ferrous Domain together with the Wargod Armor?"

"That's right."

The war god nodded: "Because the Wargod Armor is only missing one Jin of Ferrous Domain to use the Dragon's Finishing Touch, the degree of need it has for the Ferrous Domain, will drive it to devour the Ferrous Domain of its own accord."

"Of course, the moment the Wargod Armor finishes swallowing the Ferrous Domain, you will need to separate a strand of God Soul and integrate it into the Wargod Armor's Artifact Spirit."

"At that time, you can control the Wargod Armor as you wish!"

"Alright, then I'll make my move now."

Cheng Feng never thought that smelting Ferrous Domain would be so easy.

After hearing War God's explanation, he immediately began to move.

He threw the Buddha awl that he had taken from the top of the main hall of the Leiyin Temple onto the Wargod Armor's helmet.
