Tens Of Thousands Worlds Of Martial God

C1620 Panic-escape

Time flowed on. Another four or five hours passed.

Finally, Cheng Feng and the others caught up to a group of Spatial Barrier.

"Master, the aura of the Ambrosia is within this group of Spatial Barrier."

Xiaojin said as he circled around the Spatial Barrier for a while.

"The aura of the Ambrosia is inside the Spatial Barrier?"

Hearing this, Cheng Feng's brows twitched: "If that's the case, then Infinite God King might be in this plane wrapped in Spatial Barrier."

Thinking about it, Cheng Feng raised the Devil-beheading Knife up.

Buzz buzz …

At the end of the void, large stars began to light up one after another. The number of stars actually reached one hundred and forty.

On the way to Infinite God King, Cheng Feng obviously did not stay idle.

The God Soul emitted, communicated with the Nine Heavens Star, and lit up the Starlight.

The number of stars that allowed him to communicate reached 140.

Therefore, when Cheng Feng used Fifth Form of The Azure Emperor'S Saber Technique once again, Boom...

The blade descended and immediately cut a hole in the Spatial Barrier.

Soon after, Cheng Feng kept the Devil-beheading Knife and went back into the hole he made with the Spatial Barrier.

The place that the Spatial Barrier covered was vast and sparsely populated.

As Cheng Feng entered, his gaze swept around and he clearly saw the general situation of the entire plane.

"This plane should only be a small plane embedded in the Earth Sky Continent."

Cheng Feng muttered: "There are no humans living in there, only a few Demonic Beast races."

"Even the highest ranked Demonic Beast only seems to be at the fourth or fifth stage of Earth Scale. They pose no threat to me."

"Therefore, I do not need to consider the threat from this plane. I just need to focus on tracking the Infinite God King."

Thinking about it, Cheng Feng indicated for the Heavenly Dragon Louse Xiao Jin to follow him.

Receiving Cheng Feng's orders, the Xiaojin immediately took action.

His gaze quickly shifted as he flew towards the west.

"The Infinite God King is in the west."

Seeing that, Cheng Feng followed behind Xiaojin without any hesitation.

When the Emperor Clam saw this, it also flew towards the west …

At the same time, tens of thousands of miles to the west of this plane, a large river surged.

This river originated from a vast mountain, flowing through a mountain ridge and forming a huge waterfall that was over a hundred li wide.

Under the waterfall, a youth with deep eyes was carefully activating his divine power and repairing his amputated arm.

"He's fallen! The second Elder's seven clones have all fused together. He's actually fallen!"

The young man with profound eyes thought to himself, "This must be done by Cheng Feng, it's really a little scary."

"If the first person Cheng Feng pursued was not the second elder, but me, I'm afraid the person who would have fallen at this moment would be me!"

This young man was Infinite God King.

On his hands, there was a strand of the Second Elder's Spiritual Consciousness.

After the Second Elder had fallen, the first thing he had heard was that his mind had suffered a huge shock.

And then, he fled for hundreds of thousands of kilometers, hiding behind the waterfall of this unknown plane.

He concealed all of his Qi with the intention of avoiding Cheng Feng's pursuit.

However, the sense of crisis he felt in his heart was getting stronger and stronger. It was making him anxious all the time.

"Cheng Feng should be searching for my tracks right now."

Infinite God King muttered to himself, "Even though the Second Elder and I fled in reverse and even changed our position several times during this period, the other party might still catch up to us."

"In order to avoid any accidents, I must connect my severed arm as soon as possible, adjust my condition to its peak, and then leave this place as soon as possible."

Thinking about it here, Infinite God King used the method of rebirth with his severed arm.

The arm that Cheng Feng had cut off quickly produced a large amount of fresh blood and flesh, healing the wound.

However, right at this moment.

The sense of danger that he felt from the bottom of his heart suddenly increased by more than a dozen times.

This made Infinite God King's heart race, and he was about to raise his eyes to take a look.

Weng! * In the sky, the giant stars suddenly lit up.

When all the large stars reached their brightest, streams of starlight shot down and condensed into Unparalleled Knife Light, fiercely slashing down from the sky.

Sizzle …

The sky shattered and the ground split apart.

The river, which was over a hundred miles wide, rolled up from the center to both sides.

He could clearly see the mud at the bottom of the river.

The Infinite God King that was hiding behind the waterfall was immediately exposed in the air, and his face immediately paled.

"Cheng Feng? You actually caught up so quickly? "

The Infinite God King's heart surged, the aura of death rushing towards him.

Never would he have thought that Cheng Feng's speed would actually be so fast.

But in the midst of his shock, Infinite God King didn't slow down in the slightest.

He reached out his perfectly fine hand and pointed it at the Knife Light that was coming his way.

Buzz …

A steel ruler appeared out of thin air and transformed into a thousand meter long iron wall, blocking the Knife Light's explosive slash.

This iron ruler was a imitation of the Holy Artifact.

This ruler contained a wisp of The Law of Space s, which were limitless and could isolate all attacks.

Any attack aimed at the Infinitesimal Ruler would be engulfed by the infinitely stretched void.

When the Unparalleled Knife Light that Cheng Feng slashed out arrived, even though the iron ruler was right before his eyes, it was like he was separated by the ends of the earth, unable to slash even a single inch of the Infinitesimal Ruler.

"Dicang Sabreplay, cut again!"

Seeing this, the big star in the sky lit up, and another Unparalleled Knife Light fell.

When the two Knife Light s slashed into the space covered by the Infinitesimal Ruler at the same time, the Infinitesimal Ruler s instantly let out an explosive sound and began to crack.

It was obvious that he was barely holding on to the strike of the Unparalleled Knife Light, and was about to collapse.

After all, this iron ruler was not the real Holy Artifact, it was just a copy with a limited power.

As a result, he was able to hold on for less than two seconds.

The entire steel ruler was blown to smithereens.

Following that, the two remaining Knife Light s slashed onto the wide waterfall with a loud bang.

After the waterfall disappeared, the towering mountain ridge instantly collapsed, revealing a long and narrow groove that caused chills to run down one's spine.

However, Cheng Feng's wave of attacks did not kill Infinite God King.

The moment the Infinitesimal Ruler blocked the Knife Light, the other party immediately used an inch of the earth shrinking technique and fled into the distance.

"Infinite God King, don't even think of escaping!"

But right at that moment, the Emperor Clam just happened to arrive.

Seeing the fleeing Infinite God King, Spider spat out a line of transparent spider silk and suddenly rushed towards him.

As for the Emperor Frog s, they were even more straightforward.

His huge stomach bulged, his mouth aimed straight at Infinite God King, and immediately released a sound wave.

"Heaven and Earth are limitless!"

Facing the Emperor Clam's attack, Infinite God King put both of his hands together and drew a circle in front of him.

Buzz …

The spider silk that Spider spat out and the sonic wave pillar shot out by the Emperor Frog were swept away by the circle and were lured to one side.

However, this move also made Infinite God King feel bad, and his face suddenly paled.

Furthermore, Cheng Feng had already raised his Devil-beheading Knife and was about to use the third move, the Dicang Sabreplay.

"Space Split!"

Seeing that, the Infinite God King felt a chill in his heart.

Not daring to be negligent at all, he stabbed his hands into the air and used a Spatial Martial Skills, ripping the Spatial Barrier apart.

In the next moment, he jumped in and fled.
