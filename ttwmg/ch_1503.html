C1503 Sunstone

He just got rid of the one-eyed killer.

Cheng Feng's gaze swept across them and discovered that one of them, who was pale white without a trace of a hitman, had actually pounced towards a fiery-red stone.

It was as if it wanted to set the stone ablaze.

"Cheng Feng, quickly get rid of that killer."

Nalan Changsheng's voice sounded. "That fiery-red stone, seems to be a sun stone."

"This stone absorbs a huge amount of the sun's energy. Once it detonates, it is enough to kill Martial Cultivator!"

Hearing this, Cheng Feng's eyelids jumped.

The Devil-beheading Knife took the chance and killed the killer before he could detonate the sunstone.

Then, the Devil-beheading Knife swept across and killed all the remaining assassins.

After killing all the killer Instructors, Cheng Feng looked at the sun stone.

"This stone contains a terrifying energy."

Cheng Feng muttered: "If it explodes, it can indeed threaten the Sixth Level of Heaven-ascending Expert."

"It should be the Hades' Killer, something to be used to perish together with the enemy."

"Since we've met, we might as well pack it up and take it away."

While speaking, Cheng Feng activated his Dragon Strength and took the sun stone off the stage.

After observing it for a bit, he placed it into the Soul Ring.

"Cheng Feng, you sure are violent."

Just as Cheng Feng put the sunstone away, Long Aotian's voice rang out, "To think that even the Steel Fortress formed from black profound steel was penetrated through!"

Following the voice, the young man dressed in golden clothes, Long Aotian, immediately appeared outside the control room.

At the same time, there was also the Heavenly Dragon Louse Xiao Jin.

"Although the Black Black Mystical Steel is hard, it is far from being comparable to my Devil-beheading Knife."

Cheng Feng lowered his head and swept a glance at the Devil-beheading Knife, and his face revealed a trace of a smile.

The sharpness of Devil-beheading Knife s was indeed no small matter.

According to Cheng Feng's conjecture, the Devil-beheading Knife could be a Intermediate Sacristy at its peak condition.

If Cheng Feng could completely repair the Devil-beheading Knife's injuries and reconstruct its body, it would definitely be a Power.

With such a large Earth Sky Continent, he could let Cheng Feng run rampant!

"This blade of yours is quite interesting."

Long Aotian glanced at the Devil-beheading Knife, and then turned to the Serrated Eye's control room: "But compared to the blade, I like this Ultimate Mechanical Puppet more."

While speaking, Long Aotian stepped into the control room and went straight for the control board.

"It is truly hard to imagine that you humans can actually make dead things like steel into living beings that can move freely."

Long Aotian caressed the Serrated Eye, and said with a face full of shock: "And this kind of living thing, its fighting strength is super strong!"

"If we can have a few hundred of these Mechanical Puppet, then none of us need to be afraid!"

"God kill god, Buddha block!"

"Long Aotian, stop daydreaming, it's impossible."

Cheng Feng shook his head: "Ultimate Mechanical Puppet, the price for each one is exceptionally high."

"Even if your Blood God Cult is rich and imposing, you can't buy more than a few hundred of them."

Cheng Feng was not lying.

It had to be known that Serrated Eye alone had consumed a huge amount of black profound steel.

Forget about a few hundred of them, even dozens of them could completely empty a First-Class Sect's wealth!

Furthermore, Ultimate Mechanical Puppet are not something that can be bought just because you want to buy them.

Presumably, even with the great clans such as Gong Cheng and Ju Lu, building one would require a lot of time and manpower, and they wouldn't be able to produce it in bulk.

Otherwise, the entire Earth Sky Continent would have been occupied by these two great clan, and there would be no way for it to bounce back.

"There is nothing in this world that is impossible."

Long Aotian laughed, then used his hand to support the Serrated Eye: "For example, right now, don't we already have our first one?"

"As long as we continue to collect them, there will definitely be several hundred or even several thousand."

"You're thinking too much."

Cheng Feng shook his head: "We cannot use this Ultimate Mechanical Puppet."

"Can't use it? Why can't you use it? "

Long Aotian frowned: "This Mechanical Puppet was only pierced by you. It can be used to repair it."

"I'm not talking about the damage caused by the Mechanical Puppet, but about the right to use it."

Cheng Feng explained: "The moment this Mechanical Puppet was created by the Public Transport Family, it had already been labelled with a 'Hell Sect'.

"Other than those with Hell Sect, no one else can use it."

"Public Transport Family? Special label? "

Hearing that, Long Aotian lowered his head and looked.

Astonishingly, he discovered the clan emblem of the Public Transport Family on the control board.

This meant that this Mechanical Puppet was created by Public Transport Family.

"I don't believe that a mere Mechanical Puppet can recognize a person."

Long Aotian observed the control board for a moment, not believing what he saw.

He immediately grabbed the control stick on the control board with the intent of activating the Serrated Eye.

However, it was of no use at all.

Whether he pushed the lever in that direction or not, it was all useless.

Obviously, it was as Cheng Feng had expected. This Ultimate Mechanical Puppet had already been labeled with a Hell Sect.

Other than those with Hell Sect, no one else could use it.

"Enough, Long Aotian."

Glancing at the Long Aotian who had an expression of unwillingness on his face, Cheng Feng said, "If the Ultimate Mechanical Puppet could be easily snatched away by others, then it wouldn't be a Ultimate Mechanical Puppet anymore."

"Hell Sect will not spend such a large price to purchase it back."

"Damn, this is too unlucky!"

Long Aotian said in a bad mood: "It wasn't easy to get such a treasure, could it be that I have to leave it for Hell Sect?"

"Not really."

Cheng Feng laughed: "This Ultimate Mechanical Puppet, its entire body is made of rare materials, it can be said to be a huge mine."

"My Devil-beheading Knife blade is damaged, I can use this to repair it."

"Repairing your knife?"

Hearing that, Long Aotian's eyes stared: "Damn, your blade is actually damaged?"

"Since the damaged state is already so strong, if the repair is complete, wouldn't that mean …"

Thinking about it, Long Aotian's scalp turned numb.

As for Cheng Feng, he was completely unmoved, and took out the Devil-beheading Knife.

"Chi!" He inserted it into the Serrated Eye.

Buzz …

Then, the Devil-beheading Knife stabbed into the Serrated Eye.

A black mist suddenly appeared, enveloping the location where the blade had stabbed into.

Long Aotian's gaze swept across the area and immediately saw that in the area where the Serrated Eye was covered, a large amount of its essence was being absorbed by the black mist.

In just a few seconds, the black profound steel luster within a radius of a dozen meters disappeared as if it had decayed.

Long Aotian released a surge of energy and gently touched that area.

Puff! That area looked like it was made of clay, and was instantly smashed into smithereens!

"Fuck, what's going on?"

Seeing that, Long Aotian's eyes widened, and he said in shock: "Isn't that blade of yours too evil? It can actually absorb the essence of the Black Black Profound Steel! "

But Cheng Feng was used to it and said indifferently.

"There's nothing to be surprised about. This is just a self-recovery ability of the Demon Slaying Sect."
